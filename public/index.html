The Town Gates

You are the gatekeeper of a small, frightened town. Plague and dark forces close in from all sides. Every soul that passes through your gate is your responsibility.

Your People

Watch the population counter carefully. Your town holds humans, the infected, and the possessed. Let the wrong ones in and the balance shifts. Lose everyone and it is over.

Inspect & Decide

Six travelers arrive each day. Use your lantern to check papers, holy water to test for possession, medicinal herbs to detect infection. Then choose — let them in, turn them away, or execute them.

Nothing Is Certain

Travelers lie. Papers can be forged. Some hide what they are. Your supplies are limited — use them wisely. Every decision carries a cost, and reputation follows you whether you want it to or not.

You Are Not Alone

A companion will travel with you through these dark days. Choose wisely. They will not judge your decisions — but they will witness every one of them.
